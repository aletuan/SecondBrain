# Wiki log

Append-only timeline for ingests and wiki work. **Agents:** add a new `##` entry for each meaningful event. **Format** (grep-friendly):

```text
## [YYYY-MM-DD] ingest | Short title
- Capture: `Captures/YYYY-MM-DD--slug--id/` (or URL)
- Wiki: updated [[topics/…]] …

## [YYYY-MM-DD] query | Short title
- Result: [[synthesis/…]] or updated [[topics/…]]
```

---

## [YYYY-MM-DD] seed | Wiki template installed

- Initialized `Wiki/` tree from Brain `vault-template` (no capture yet).
